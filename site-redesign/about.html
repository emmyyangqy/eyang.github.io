About Red Moth Jewelry

RED MOTH JEWELRY'S MISSION:

MORE THAN METAL AND STONE

Red Moth Jewelry is a small business that provides unique, handmade jewelry out of precious metals and semi-precious gemstones. Each piece is designed and crafted by hand, all by a single person. Red Moth Jewelry strives to provide size-inclusive, one-of-a-kind statement jewelry inspired by folklore and story-telling. Each piece is fabricated with love and intention, and aims to provide a little extra magic to the person that wears it.

THE FACE BEHIND RED MOTH:

A METAL MELTER FROM THE MIDWEST

Red Moth Jewelry is a one-woman operation based out of Central/Southern Indiana. Sara is the creator, designer, and metalsmith behind Red Moth Jewelry. She is self-taught and started experimenting with silversmithing in 2018, inspired by her longtime love of turquoise, statement rings, and all things metal. She graduated from Indiana University with a BFA in Photography, a BA in Art History, and a minor in Folklore and Ethnomusicology. Her studies of fine art, art history, folklore, and story-telling heavily influence her jewelry. Her fascination with the paranormal, and all things dark and macabre, greatly inspire her artistic style. She lives and practices in Southern Indiana with her husband and two Italian Greyhounds. She enjoys spending time outdoors, traveling, and watching horror movies, but her one of her greatest joys is crafting unique, size-inclusive jewelry for anyone/everyone that wants to rock it.
